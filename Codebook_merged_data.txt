Codebook for merged_train_test_data.txt and Suject_Activity_Means.txt

This file explains what each of the variables are in the merged_train_test_data.txt and the Subject_Activity_Means.txt files.

Subjects - A unique identifier for each individual participating in the study.
activity_code - 1: Walking; 2: Walking Upstairs; 3: Walking Downstairs; 4: Sitting; 5: Standing; 6: Laying
tBodyAcc-mean()-X - Mean of the total body acceleration in the x-axis
tBodyAcc-mean()-Y - Mean of the total body acceleration in the y-axis
tBodyAcc-mean()-Z - Mean of the total body acceleration in the z-axis
tBodyAcc-std()-X - Standard deviation of the total body acceleration in the x-axis
tBodyAcc-std()-Y - Standard deviation of the total body acceleration in the y-axis
tBodyAcc-std()-Z - Standard deviation of the total body acceleration in the z-axis 
tGravityAcc-mean()-X - Mean of the total gravity acceleration in the x-axis
tGravityAcc-mean()-Y - Mean of the total gravity acceleration in the y-axis
tGravityAcc-mean()-Z - Mean of the total gravity acceleration in the z-axis
tGravityAcc-std()-X - Standard deviation of the total gravity acceleration in the x-axis
tGravityAcc-std()-Y - Standard deviation of the total gravity acceleration in the y-axis
tGravityAcc-std()-Z - Standard deviation of the total gravity acceleration in the z-axis
tBodyAccJerk-mean()-X - Mean of the the total body acceleration jerk in the x-axis 
tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Y - Mean of the the total body acceleration jerk in the y-axis
tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Z - Mean of the the total body acceleration jerk in the z-axis
tBodyAccJerk-std()-X - Standard deviaton of the total body acceleration jerk in the x-axis
tBodyAccJerk-std()-Y - Standard deviaton of the total body acceleration jerk in the y-axis
tBodyAccJerk-std()-Z - Standard deviaton of the total body acceleration jerk in the z-axis
tBodyGyro-mean()-X - Mean of the total body gyroscope measurement in the x-axis
tBodyGyro-mean()-Y - Mean of the total body gyroscope measurement in the y-axis
tBodyGyro-mean()-Z - Mean of the total body gyroscope measurement in the z-axis
tBodyGyro-std()-X - Standard deviation of the total body gyroscope measurement in the x-axis
tBodyGyro-std()-Y - Standard deviation of the total body gyroscope measurement in the y-axis
tBodyGyro-std()-Z - Standard deviation of the total body gyroscope measurement in the z-axis
tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-X - Mean of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement in the x-axis
tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-Y - Mean of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement in the y-axis
tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-Z - Mean of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement in the z-axis
tBodyGyroJerk-std()-X - Standard deviation of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement in the x-axis
tBodyGyroJerk-std()-Y - Standard deviation of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement in the y-axis
tBodyGyroJerk-std()-Z - Standard deviation of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement in the z-axis
tBodyAccMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the total body acceleration 
tBodyAccMag-std() - Standard deviation of the magnitude of the total body acceleration 
tGravityAccMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the total gravity acceleration 
tGravityAccMag-std() - Standard deviation of the magnitude of the total gravity acceleration
tBodyAccJerkMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the total body jerk acceleration
tBodyAccJerkMag-std() - Standard deviation of the magnitude of the total body jerk acceleration
tBodyGyroMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the total body gyroscope measurement
tBodyGyroMag-std() - Standard deviation of the magnitude of the total body gyroscope measurement
tBodyGyroJerkMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement
tBodyGyroJerkMag-std() - Standard deviation of the magnitude of the total body gyroscope jerk measurement
fBodyAcc-mean()-X - Mean of the filtered body acceleration in the x-axis
fBodyAcc-mean()-Y - Mean of the filtered body acceleration in the y-axis
fBodyAcc-mean()-Z - Mean of the filtered body acceleration in the z-axis
fBodyAcc-std()-X - Standard deviation of the filtered body acceleration in the x-axis
fBodyAcc-std()-Y - Standard deviation of the filtered body acceleration in the y-axis
fBodyAcc-std()-Z - Standard deviation of the filtered body acceleration in the z-axis
fBodyAccJerk-mean()-X - Mean of the filtered body jerk acceleration in the x-axis 
fBodyAccJerk-mean()-Y - Mean of the filtered body jerk acceleration in the y-axis
fBodyAccJerk-mean()-Z - Mean of the filtered body jerk acceleration in the z-axis
fBodyAccJerk-std()-X - Standard deviation of the filtered body jerk acceleration in the x-axis
fBodyAccJerk-std()-Y - Standard deviation of the filtered body jerk acceleration in the y-axis
fBodyAccJerk-std()-Z - Standard deviation of the filtered body jerk acceleration in the z-axis
fBodyGyro-mean()-X - Mean of the filtered body gyroscope measurement in the x-axis 
fBodyGyro-mean()-Y - Mean of the filtered body gyroscope measurement in the y-axis
fBodyGyro-mean()-Z - Mean of the filtered body gyroscope measurement in the z-axis
fBodyGyro-std()-X - Standard deviation of the filtered body gyroscope measurement in the x-axis
fBodyGyro-std()-Y - Standard deviation of the filtered body gyroscope measurement in the y-axis
fBodyGyro-std()-Z - Standard deviation of the filtered body gyroscope measurement in the z-axis
fBodyAccMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the filtered body acceleration
fBodyAccMag-std() - Standard deviation of the magnitude of the filtered body acceleration
fBodyBodyAccJerkMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the filtered body jerk acceleration
fBodyBodyAccJerkMag-std() - Standard deviation of the magnitude of the filtered body jerk acceleration
fBodyBodyGyroMag-mean() - Mean of the magnitude of the filtered body gyroscope measurement
